Hi, I’m
Shubhanshu Gupta

PhD Student, Aerospace Engineering

I am a PhD scholar in Aerospace Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur, advised by Prof. Mangal Kothari and Prof. Abhishek . My research focuses on the dynamics, control, and optimization of hybrid UAVs, with a special emphasis on the biplane tailsitter configuration. Broadly, I am interested in developing efficient transition trajectory optimization methods and advanced control strategies for improving the performance of next-generation hybrid aerial vehicles.
